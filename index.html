ICT TRADING · SMART MONEY CONCEPTS CH.01 — CANDLESTICK
── មេរៀនទី ១ ──
Candlestick — រចនាសម្ព័ន្ធ Candle
Candlestick Anatomy
១.១ Candlestick ជាអ្វី?

Candlestick ជា Bar ដែលបង្ហាញ Open, High, Low, Close (OHLC) នៃ Price ក្នុង Time Period មួយ ។ ICT Trader ប្រើ Candlestick ដើម្បីអាន Price Action ។

រូបភាព: Bullish (Green) & Bearish (Red) — Open, High, Low, Close
ចំណុចសំខាន់
  • Open — តម្លៃពេល Candle ចាប់ផ្ដើម
  • Close — តម្លៃពេល Candle បញ្ចប់
  • High — តម្លៃខ្ពស់បំផុត (Upper Wick)
  • Low — តម្លៃទាបបំផុត (Lower Wick)
  • Body — ចន្លោះ Open & Close
  • Wick — Tail ខាងលើ/ក្រោម Body
★ Bullish Candle (Green): Close > Open = Buyer Win ។ Bearish Candle (Red): Close < Open = Seller Win ។ Candle ម្នាក់ឯងគ្មានន័យ — ត្រូវ Read ជាមួយ Context (Structure + Zone + Session) ។

ICT TRADING · SMART MONEY CONCEPTS CH.02 — MARKET STRUCTURE
── មេរៀនទី ២ ──
Market Structure — BOS & CHoCH
Market Structure
២.១ Market Structure

Market Structure = Pattern នៃ HH/HL (Bullish) ឬ LH/LL (Bearish) ។ ICT Trader អាន Structure ដើម្បីដឹង Direction ។

Bearish Structure (LH/LL) → CHoCH → Bullish Structure (HH/HL) — BOS Confirmed

BEARISH STRUCTURE

  • Lower High (LH) — High ទូស្ស គ្មានថ្លៃ High មុន
  • Lower Low (LL) — Low ទាបជាង Low មុន
  • Trend: Price ចុះក្រោម

BULLISH STRUCTURE

  • Higher High (HH) — High ខ្ពស់ជាង High មុន
  • Higher Low (HL) — Low ខ្ពស់ជាង Low មុន
  • Trend: Price ឡើងលើ
CHoCH & BOS

CHoCH (Change of Character) = Signal ដំបូង ដែល Trend អាច Reverse ។ Price Break ខ្ពស់ជាង Previous Swing High = CHoCH Bullish ។

BOS (Break of Structure) = Confirm ថា New Trend ពិតប្រាកដ ។ ក្រោយ CHoCH: Price បង្កើត HH & HL = BOS Bullish Confirmed ។

★ CHoCH = First Signal (Weak Signal) ។ BOS = Confirmation (Strong Signal) ។ Trade ត្រូវ Wait BOS ជា Rule ។ Never Enter on CHoCH alone ។

── មេរៀនទី ៣ ──
Liquidity — SSL & BSL Sweep
Liquidity Concepts

Liquidity = Stop Loss Orders ដែល Retail Traders ដាក់ ។ Smart Money ត្រូវការ Liquidity ដើម្បី Fill Orders ធំ ។ Price ចូលចិត្ត Seek Liquidity មុន Reverse ។

SSL Sweep (ខាងក្រោម Lows) → Reversal↑  |  BSL Sweep (ខាងលើ Highs) → Reversal↓

SELL SIDE LIQUIDITY (SSL)

  • SL របស់ Long Buyers Below Swing Lows
  • Price Sweep SSL = ចុះ → Grab Stops → Reverse ឡើង
  • ដូចជា: Stop Hunt Below Low

BUY SIDE LIQUIDITY (BSL)

  • SL របស់ Short Sellers Above Swing Highs
  • Price Sweep BSL = ឡើង → Grab Stops → Reverse ចុះ
  • ដូចជា: Stop Hunt Above High
★ Price Always Seeks Liquidity ។ Retail Traders ជា Victim ។ ចូរ Think: "Price ទៅ Hunt Stops ឯណា?" ជំនួស "Price ទៅណា?"

ICT TRADING · SMART MONEY CONCEPTS CH.04 — ORDER BLOCK
── មេរៀនទី ៤ ──
Order Block (OB)
Order Block

Order Block = Candle ចុងក្រោយ ផ្ទុយទិស មុន Impulsive Move ធំ ។ Zone OB = ទីកន្លែង ដែល Smart Money ដាក់ Orders ។

Bullish OB = Last Bearish Candle មុន Impulse Up → Retest OB Zone → BUY Entry

BULLISH ORDER BLOCK

  • Last Red Candle មុន Strong Bullish Impulse
  • Zone: Open to Close of Bearish Candle
  • Wait Retest → BUY Entry
  • SL: Below OB Low

BEARISH ORDER BLOCK

  • Last Green Candle មុន Strong Bearish Impulse
  • Zone: Open to Close of Bullish Candle
  • Wait Retest → SELL Entry
  • SL: Above OB High
OB Confluence Rule
  • Liquidity Swept ជាមុន (SSL ឬ BSL Sweep)
  • ស្ថិតក្នុង HTF Premium/Discount Zone
  • Align ជាមួយ FVG ឬ FVG ស្ថិតក្នុង OB
  • BOS ឬ CHoCH Confirmed ក្រោយ Retest
★ OB + Liquidity Swept + FVG + Kill Zone = High Probability Setup ។ OB គ្មាន Confluence = Weak Zone ។
ICT TRADING · SMART MONEY CONCEPTS CH.05 — FAIR VALUE GAP
── មេរៀនទី ៥ ──
Fair Value Gap (FVG)
Fair Value Gap

FVG = Zone ដែល Price Move ឆ្ងាយបស្ចាយ ដែល Leave Inefficiency (Gap) ។ Price ចូលចិត្ត Come Back Fill Gap ។ FVG = Imbalance Zone — Market ត្រូវ Balance ។

Bullish FVG (Buy Zone) | Bearish FVG (Sell Zone) | CE = Midpoint 50% = Magnet Level
FVG — 3 Candle Pattern
  • Candle 1 — High (Bullish FVG) ឬ Low (Bearish FVG)
  • Candle 2 — Impulse Candle ធំ Strong Move
  • Candle 3 — Low (Bullish) ឬ High (Bearish)
  • FVG Zone — ចន្លោះ Wick C1 & Wick C3
CE & OTE
  • CE = Midpoint (50%) FVG = Magnet Level
  • Price ច្រើន Touch CE ហើយ Reverse
  • OTE = FVG + Fib 61.8–79% + OB
  • BPR = Overlap Bullish+Bearish FVG = ខ្លាំង
★ Best Entry = ចូល ក្នុង FVG + Fibonacci 61.8–79% Zone + OB Confluence = Optimal Trade Entry (OTE) ។
ICT TRADING · SMART MONEY CONCEPTS CH.06 — POWER OF 3
── មេរៀនទី ៦ ──
Power of 3 — AMD Model
Power of 3 (AMD)

Power of 3 = AMD Model ។ Market ក្នុង 1 ថ្ងៃ ដំណើរការ ៣ Phase: Accumulation → Manipulation → Distribution ។

Asia (Accumulation A) → London Manipulation M (SSL Sweep) → NY Distribution D ↑

A — ACCUMULATION (ASIA)

  • 07:00–15:00 PP Time
  • Price Consolidate — Range Build
  • BSL (Above) + SSL (Below) ត្រូវបាន Mark
  • Avoid Trade ក្នុង Asia

M+D — MANIPULATION & DISTRIBUTION

  • London 15:00: False Move — Sweep SSL/BSL
  • Retail Traders Trapped
  • NY 20:30: True Direction — Smart Money Distribute
  • TP = Opposite Liquidity
★ Watch Asia Range → Wait London Sweep SSL ឬ BSL → Enter ក្រោយ Sweep & CHoCH → TP = NY Session Distribution ។
ICT TRADING · SMART MONEY CONCEPTS CH.07 — ICT FULL TRADE SETUP
── មេរៀនទី ៧ ──
ICT Full Trade Setup — XAUUSD
ICT Full Trade Setup
ICT Full Setup: SSL Sweep → CHoCH → OB/FVG Entry → SL Below Sweep → TP = BSL Zone (RRR 1:2.4+)
Step-by-Step Trade Plan
  • HTF Bias: 4H Bullish Structure Confirmed
  • Identify Asia Session High/Low → BSL & SSL
  • London Open: Price Sweeps SSL (ចុះក្រោម Asia Low)
  • Price Reversal → CHoCH Candle Break Above Previous High
  • LTF (5M/15M): Find OB ឬ FVG ក្រោយ CHoCH
  • Entry ក្នុង OB/FVG Zone (Optimal Trade Entry)
  • SL: Below SSL Sweep Low (Safe Buffer)
  • TP: BSL Level (Asia High ឬ Previous Swing High)
★ Setup នេះ Give RRR 1:2 ទៅ 1:5 ជាធម្មតា ។ Key = Patience + Wait Confirmation ។ Never Enter Before CHoCH ។

Lot Size Formula
Lot Size = (Account Balance × Risk%) ÷ (SL Distance × $10/pip)
Daily Loss Limit
  • ប្រសិន Loss ដល់ 3% ក្នុង 1 ថ្ងៃ → Stop Trading
  • Revenge Trading = Account Killer
  • Log ហើយ Review ។ Trade Again Next Day
Drawdown Rules
  • Max DD 10% = Reduce Lot Size 50%
  • Max DD 20% = Back to Demo
  • Never Double Down to Recover
★ Protect Capital First ។ Small Consistent Gains > One Big Win ។ Define SL ជាមុន ហើយ Calculate Lot Size ។ Never Reverse ។

Trading Psychology
Trading Psychology

Strategy + Risk Management + Psychology = ៣ សសរ ទ្រ Trading ។ Psychology ១ ក្នុង ៣ = ការ Control Emotion ។

PROBLEMS TRADERS FACE

  • FOMO — Chase Price = Loss
  • Revenge Trading — Open ក្រោយ Loss ដោយ Emotion
  • Overconfidence — Over-Leverage ក្រោយ Win
  • Impulsive Entry — Enter Without Confirmation

DISCIPLINE RULES

  • Follow Trading Plan 100%
  • Accept Loss as Normal Cost
  • Keep Trade Journal (Record Everything)
  • Minimum 6 Months Backtesting
